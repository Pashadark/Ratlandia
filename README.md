# 🐀 Ratlandia — Telegram RPG Bot v5.0

**A full-scale RPG set in the underground world of rats and mice, featuring Mafia-style social deduction, PvE dungeon crawling, crafting, clans, and achievements.**

## 🎮 GAME MODES

### 🔪 MAFIA (The Rat)
Classic social deduction for 3–10 players. The Rat hunts at night, the Mice vote by day.
- 🌙 Night phase with victim selection
- 💬 Daytime discussion
- 🗳️ Voting and execution
- 🎒 Consumables and abilities during matches

### 🕳️ TUNNELS (PvE)
Solo dungeon runs with progressive difficulty.
- 👹 **20+ monsters** with unique abilities
- 👑 **Bosses** and elite enemies
- 👥 **Co-op mode** — fight bosses together
- ✨ **Blessings** — run-long buffs
- 🎲 Dice rolls for fleeing

### 🔨 BLACKSMITH (Crafting)
Craft items from resources dropped by monsters.
- 📜 **Recipes** — loot from enemies
- 🎲 **2d6 roll** determines quality
- 💎 9 quality tiers (Terrible → Divine)
- 🌟 Critical crafts, cursed recipes, double crafts
- 🔮 Fortune teller — quality prediction

### 🏰 CITY
- 🏪 **Shop** — buy and sell items
- ⛪ **Church** — healing and recovery
- 🍺 **Tavern** — dice games, beer, tournaments
- 🛡️ **Clans** — create, manage, leaderboards
